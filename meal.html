Choose Your Preferred Meal

Enjoy your preferred meal at no extra charge.

No Preference

This option indicates the passenger has no specific dietary restrictions or requirements. The airline may serve the standard in-flight meal available to all passengers.

Halal Meal

Prepared according to Islamic dietary laws, halal meals use ingredients and cooking methods permitted. They exclude pork, alcohol, and ensure meat is sourced in a prescribed manner.

Kosher Meal

Kosher meals follow Jewish dietary laws, ensuring food is prepared under rabbinical supervision. They exclude pork, shellfish, and prevent the mixing of meat and dairy products.

Vegetarian Meal

A vegetarian meal contains no meat, fish, or poultry and focuses on plant-based ingredients like vegetables, grains, and legumes. It is suitable for passengers who prefer or require a meat-free diet.
